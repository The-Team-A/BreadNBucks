    ABOUT

    National Stock Exchange has a total market capitalization of more than US$1.41 trillion, making it the world’s 12th-largest stock exchange as of March 2016. The National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE) is the leading stock exchange of India, located in Mumbai.Nifty is a major stock index in India introduced by the National stock exchange. The NSE is situated at Delhi. The Sensex and Nifty are both Indices. The Sensex, also called the BSE 30, is a stock market index of 30 well-established and financially sound companies listed on Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). The Nifty, similarly, is an indicator of the 50 top major companies on the National Stock Exchange (NSE). The Nifty 50 was a group of 50 stocks that were most favored by institutional investors in the 1960s and 1970s. Companies in this group were usually characterized by consistent earnings growth and high P/E ratios.
